Sica are a 5-piece coming together of minds, collaborators and former bands that have been co-conspiring to melt your face with tasty riffage since early 2023.

Influences range from metal your granny secretly moshes when pre-drinking before the bingo, to brand new vibes only played by tiny japanese girls on tiktok. There’s something for everyone, like it or not.

With the release of our Debut EP “Late to the Party”, we've seen extensive radio play on Radio Nova, and have started a series of gigs and collaborations over the course of 2024.

Síca are:

Paul Walker: Vocals

Tony Murray: Lead Guitar

Ken Seery: Rhythm Guitar

Dave O'Connor: Bass/Vocals

Tommy Byrne: Drums
